# 3D backgrounds for dashboard cards

Десять полноформатных фонов созданы встроенным генератором изображений Codex и оптимизированы в WebP 1536×1024. Текст и интерфейсные элементы в изображения не вшиты. Старые тёмные фоны объектов сохранены как резерв, активная светлая серия имеет суффикс `-v2`.

## Prompt set

### Residential

Premium cinematic 3D architectural visualization for a construction management app object card. A modern residential high-rise under construction at blue dawn, exposed concrete floors, warm work lights in a few openings, one elegant tower crane and subtle scaffolding. Composition is wide and editorial: preserve a large calm dark navy negative-space area on the left for white UI text, place the detailed building and crane mostly on the right. Deep blue, cyan and restrained copper-orange palette, realistic physically based materials, volumetric haze, sophisticated depth, premium architectural visualization, clean and uncluttered. No people in foreground, no logos, no lettering, no numbers, no UI, no frame, no watermark.
